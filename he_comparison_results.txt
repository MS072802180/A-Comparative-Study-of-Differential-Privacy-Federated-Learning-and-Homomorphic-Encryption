NON-LINEAR MODEL (MLP with xĠ activation):

COMMON METRICS:
  Encryption time per sample: 5.49 ms

CONCLUSIONS:
  - Non-linear model achieves much higher accuracy (69.6% vs 30.5%)
  - Non-linear HE inference is ~2.4x slower than linear HE
  - Both preserve accuracy exactly (CKKS is exact for these ops)
  - Trade-off: higher accuracy costs more computation time
