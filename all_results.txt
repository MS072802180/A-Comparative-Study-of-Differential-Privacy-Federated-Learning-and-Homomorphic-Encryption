  Source: RadioML 2018.01A

  Modulation classes: 4
  SNR: 10 dB

BASELINE MODEL:

FEDERATED LEARNING:
  Using FedAvg with 10 rounds, 3 local epochs
  Centralized baseline: 0.9112
  Clients -> Accuracy:
    2 clients: 0.9200

    10 clients: 0.9039
    20 clients: 0.9069

HOMOMORPHIC ENCRYPTION:
  Scheme: CKKS via TenSEAL (linear model proof-of-concept)
  Linear model accuracy: 0.3500
  Encryption time: 2.46 ms/sample
  Decryption time: 1.00 ms/sample
  Note: CNN inference would be 100-1000x slower
  Reference: Gilad-Bachrach et al. (2016) CryptoNets

CONCLUSION:
  - Differential Privacy: strong guarantees, 15-55% accuracy loss
  - Federated Learning: minimal accuracy loss, requires distributed setup
  - Homomorphic Encryption: strongest privacy, highest computational cost
